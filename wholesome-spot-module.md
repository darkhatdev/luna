## The Philosophy

Occupational therapists use sensory gardens. Speech therapists use vocabulary drills. Neither usually talks to each other.

The Wholesome Spot combines both:
- **Sensory integration** through interactive, self-directed activities
- **Vocabulary acquisition** through context, not memorization
- **Emotional regulation** through environmental choice and control
- **Agency** â€” the user picks everything: animal, activity, language, volume, duration

This isn't a lesson with a garden theme. It's a garden where language happens to grow.
